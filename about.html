EMR Company Profile

EMR was formally registered as a business in 2011 and dealt mainly in the sourcing, distribution and retail of music, movies and video games but has since grown into also sourcing, distribution, installation and configuration of related electronic accessories such as speakers, amplifiers, lights, TVs & projectors and gaming consoles.

Divided into two divisions, EMR Consultancy is the arm of the business dealing with tailored audio visual installations of varying scales. From simple home theatre solutions or large multi room home entertainment solutions adapted for control using a user’s phone to audio, video, lights and sound proofing installations for auditoriums or complex automated multi-zone systems that could span an entire institute on multiple levels and control it’s schedules (e.g. school class times) and emergency procedures.

With more than 10 years of experience across East Africa, we bring together different aspects of the entertainment industry and through creative innovations, merge these to offer an allinclusive package that benefits our clients. With EMR you get expertise nurtured from handling and supporting installations in individual homes (Runda, Karen), in entertainment venues (Teriyaki Japan, Century Cinemas), in hotels (Flamingo Resort, Enashipai) and in learning institutions (GEM School, St Christopher’s School). We also have a useful maintenance package that along with ensuring the system installed is always working at optimum conditions, detects any issues before they can escalate into more costly bugs.

We believe that no two contracts are the same and so using our vast and varied expertise, together with skilled and specialized strategic partners, EMR is at the lead with innovations that seek to achieve our mission;

“To give timely and apt answers that go past just sating our clients’ needs but also imbue in them an almost childish delight that one can only get from enjoying something for the first time.”

We kindly invite you to allow us make your dream come true.
